4. Combining Data: &, CONCAT, TEXTJOIN
& Operator:
=A1 & " " & B1 â†’ Joins A1 and B1 with a space in between.

TEXTJOIN() Function:

Joins values with a delimiter, ignoring empty cells if TRUE.

8. Date & Time Functions
=TODAY() â†’ Returns current date.

=TODAY()-1 â†’ Yesterday's date.

=TODAY()+1 â†’ Tomorrow's date.

=NOW() â†’ Returns current date and time.

Date Components:

DATEDIF Function:
